Global Cosmetics Brand Order Management Transformation

How a leading cosmetics brand achieved unprecedented efficiency improvements through smart order management implementation.

The Challenge

The cosmetics brand was facing increasing pressure to improve order management efficiency while maintaining high customer satisfaction in a competitive market. Their existing order management processes were manual and siloed, leading to errors, delays, and poor visibility into order status.

With complex order flows across multiple channels, they needed a solution that could streamline Customer Order Capture, Order Validation, and Order Issue Management processes, reducing human error and providing real-time insights.

Additionally, they were experiencing challenges with order tracking, inventory accuracy, and timely resolution of order issues, resulting in customer complaints and lost sales.

The Solution

NTT DATA implemented a comprehensive smart order management solution that integrated Customer Order Capture, Order Validation, and Order Issue Management processes. This system provided end-to-end visibility and real-time tracking of orders.

We deployed AI-powered algorithms for automated order validation that could detect and resolve issues in real-time, reducing manual intervention. The solution also included intelligent order routing and prioritization based on business rules.

A centralized order management dashboard was created, enabling real-time monitoring of order status, inventory levels, and performance metrics. Smart analytics provided predictive insights for demand forecasting and inventory optimization.
