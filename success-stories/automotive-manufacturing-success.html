Global Automotive Manufacturer Transformation

How a leading automotive manufacturer achieved unprecedented efficiency improvements through smart manufacturing implementation.

The Challenge

The automotive manufacturer was facing increasing pressure to improve production efficiency while maintaining the highest quality standards in an increasingly competitive market. Their existing manufacturing processes were largely manual, leading to inconsistent quality and significant downtime.

With multiple production lines across several facilities, they needed a solution that could standardize operations, reduce human error, and provide real-time insights into their manufacturing processes.

Additionally, they were experiencing challenges with predictive maintenance and inventory management, resulting in unexpected equipment failures and supply chain disruptions.

The Solution

NTT DATA implemented a comprehensive smart manufacturing solution that integrated IoT sensors throughout the production line, connected to an advanced analytics platform. This system provided real-time monitoring of equipment performance, product quality, and production metrics.

We deployed AI-powered predictive maintenance algorithms that could anticipate equipment failures before they occurred, allowing for scheduled maintenance during planned downtime. The solution also included automated quality control systems using computer vision and machine learning.

A digital twin of the entire manufacturing process was created, enabling simulation and optimization of production parameters in real-time, while smart inventory management ensured optimal stock levels and timely replenishment.
