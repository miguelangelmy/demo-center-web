What is the Living Lab?

The NTT DATA Living Lab is a collaborative space where we work with clients, partners, and industry experts to co-create and test innovative solutions. Our focus areas include:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Blockchain and Distributed Ledger Technologies
  • 5G and Edge Computing
  • Sustainability and Green Technologies

Through our Living Lab, we aim to accelerate digital transformation and drive innovation across industries.

Our Capabilities

Our Living Lab is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and tools to support a wide range of innovation projects:

  • AI and ML development environments
  • IoT prototyping labs
  • Blockchain development and testing platforms
  • 5G testbeds
  • Sustainability assessment tools

Our team of experts works closely with partners to tailor solutions to specific business needs and challenges.
